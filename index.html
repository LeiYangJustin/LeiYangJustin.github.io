YANG Lei

I am a lecturer (teaching track, since 2023) at Tam Wing Fan Innovation Wing (InnoWing), Faculty of Engineering, The University of Hong Kong.

I design and develop extracurricular, experiential learning activities to provide students with hands-on experiences in various STEM-related fields. I also supervise student projects in the areas of robotics, computer vision, computer graphics, and deep learning. It is much fun to work with brilliant young minds and inspiring teachers.

My research interests include geometric design, robotics, and engineering education.
