Research Interests

I am constantly interested in developing novel geometric modeling methods to ease the users' burden in mechanical design. I have developed geometric modeling tools for rapid Finite element model adaptation, participated in a quick creation of 3D textured creatures from single-view drawings. I am working on modeling microstructures for mechanical applications.
